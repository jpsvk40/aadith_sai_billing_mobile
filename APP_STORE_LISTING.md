## Description
Aadith Sai Billing is a cloud ERP for Indian businesses — manage your billing, invoicing, inventory, purchases and field service from one app that stays in sync with your web account.

Built for owners, admins and staff who need their books in their pocket.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• GST invoicing — create tax-compliant invoices and payment receipts
• Customers & products — keep your masters up to date on the go
• Orders to dispatch — track orders through production, packing and delivery
• Inventory — check stock, locations and movements in real time
• Vendor purchases — snap a vendor bill and let the app read the line items for you
• Payments & collections — record receipts and follow up on outstanding dues
• Service & warranty — raise tickets, schedule visits, and manage AMC/service contracts
• Ask your business — get plain-English answers about your numbers

Your subscription and account are managed on our secure web portal at aadithsaibillingcloud.com. This app is a companion client for your existing Aadith Sai Cloud Billing account.

Aadith Sai Cloud Billing is a business software service. There are no in-app purchases; billing is handled entirely on the web.
